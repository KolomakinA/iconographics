The Solution: Reverse Proxy Architecture

The integration is achieved using a Reverse Proxy. This is a standard, secure, and highly effective architecture for White Label solutions. Your reverse proxy server acts as the "front door" for your domain. It receives all user traffic and intelligently forwards it to the Airalo platform, while ensuring your domain remains in the user's address bar. This process is completely invisible to the end-user.

Email Communication Setup

To maintain brand consistency, transactional emails (like order confirmations) must come from your domain. The Airalo platform uses Postmark for reliable delivery. You have two options to authorize the Airalo platform to send emails on your behalf.

Option 1: Full Domain Verification (Recommended) üëç

How it works: You add specific DNS records (DKIM and Return-Path) that Airalo provides to your domain's configuration.

This is the industry-standard method for maximum email deliverability and security. It allows the Airalo platform to send emails from any address on your domain (e.g., `support@airgsm.com`), providing full flexibility.

Option 2: Single Email Address Verification

How it works: Provide a single email address to Airalo. Postmark will send a verification link to this address, which you must click.

This is a simpler setup but is less flexible. The Airalo platform will only be able to send emails from that one exact address.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How can we manage SEO for the White Label e-shop?

This is a key benefit of the reverse proxy model. You have full control over how your domain is presented to search engines. Your proxy can manage your `robots.txt` and `sitemap.xml` files, implement 301/302 redirects, and even modify HTTP headers for advanced SEO tactics. This gives you complete authority over your search engine presence.

Q: Who is responsible for on-page SEO elements like header tags (<h1>, <h2>)?

This responsibility is shared. The Airalo White Label Storefront provides the core e-shop page with a well-structured and semantic use of header tags. Your team then controls the final output via the reverse proxy. While you can't change the platform's core HTML, you can override metadata like the page `<title>` or inject structured data (like JSON-LD for rich snippets) to enhance the base provided by Airalo.

Q: Can we use our own analytics and cookie consent tools?

Yes, and it is expected. As part of your responsibilities, you will provide the script for your cookie and consent management platform. Similarly, you can use your reverse proxy to inject your own analytics scripts (e.g., Google Analytics, Adobe Analytics). This ensures you own all your user data and remain compliant with privacy regulations like GDPR.

Q: Why is a reverse proxy necessary? Can't we just use a CNAME redirect?

A simple CNAME redirect would show the Airalo White Label Storefront's URL in the browser's address bar after the page loads, breaking the seamless brand experience. The reverse proxy is the essential component that "masks" the origin URL, keeping your domain visible at all times. This is critical for brand trust, SEO, and gives you the control over caching, security, and analytics outlined in this document.
